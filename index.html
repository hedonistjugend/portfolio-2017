üìö Aggregion autumn 2015 ‚Äì spring 2017

Content Owners have a lot of stuff: books, videos, audios, software & access to their secret services. So they want these things to be easy and quick to access by users in exchange of user's money. Definitely Content Owners are afraid that users will share content with each other without permission. Or even pirates will steal their content. So here we come: we are Aggregion and we protect Content Owner's stuff and help to distribute it to end users. Oh, and also there are Resellers, who want to sell various Content Owners' content to broader audience. And of course there are libraries and universities too, who are willing to share their books, videos etc, but need it to be returnable.

We are quite good at our job so our clients and partners include Microsoft, Samsung, Intel, Disney and many more small & big publishers and content owners.

I am Lead Product Designer (basically because I'm the only product designer) responsible for all tasks involving design: main web app, tablet apps, video players, content e-mails and so on.

EMP (which in Russian stands for United Medical Portal) helps people to make doctor appointments. This portal has full information on doctor's background, also photos of clinics and reviews. Everything to make the decision easier. Also EMP has it own bonus-system, specialised blogs and diseases' encyclopedia.

EMP works in 20 biggest russian cities and around 1000 appointments are set everyday. Recently EMP were funded by IIDF, the biggest russian startup accelerator.

I was involved in EMP's big redesign & then helped them to launch bonus system and blog.
